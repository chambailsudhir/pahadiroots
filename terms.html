1. Returns for Food / Edible Products

In accordance with hygiene, safety, and perishable goods exemptions under consumer protection laws, returns and exchanges are generally not permitted for edible items including honey, ghee, saffron, shilajit, teas, spices, and all other food products.

However, as a goodwill measure, we may consider return/refund requests for edible items on a case-to-case basis, subject to:

  • Request raised within 48 hours of delivery
  • Clear photographic or video evidence provided
  • Issue pertains to damage, spoilage, or quality concern attributable to transit or manufacturing defect

Our decision in these matters is taken in good faith and communicated transparently, without prejudice to your statutory rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 or any other applicable law.

2. Returns for Non-Edible Products

Returns are accepted for non-edible items (packaging, accessories, gift sets) where:

  • Product is defective, damaged, or not as described
  • Return request raised within 48 hours of delivery
  • Product is unused, unopened, in original condition with all packaging, labels, and accessories intact

3. Products NOT Eligible for Return

❌ The following will generally not be accepted for return or refund, except as required by applicable law:

  • Products that have been opened, used, altered, or tampered with
  • Items reported after 48 hours of delivery
  • Products without original packaging or labels
  • Items damaged due to consumer misuse or negligence
  • Products rendered unfit for inspection
  • Edible items once opened — unless a quality defect is raised within 48 hours
  • Change of mind after the order has been shipped
